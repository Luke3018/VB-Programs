The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is the third instalment in the Witcher franchise and the sequel to the Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. The game is an action, open world, RPG set in a fantasy, the Witcher 3 was developed by CD Projekt RED. The Witcher 3 was released in 2015 and won game of the year for that year as it was such a successful game. The image on the left is of the main game and the two extra DLCs (downloadable content) that can be purchased to extend the story and world of the Witcher 3. You play as Geralt of Rivia the main protagonist in the Witcher franchise and you are a highly skilled monster slayer for hire, but this time you must rescue a close friend from an uncertain fate.
